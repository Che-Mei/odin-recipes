Lemon Salmon and Brussel Sprouts

Lemon Salmon seasoned with Magic Salmon seasoning and baked brussel sprouts with olive oil drizzle and salt and pepper

This salmon dish is incredibly easy to make! It only takes about 10 minutes of prep and is a go-to dinner for weeknights. Get your oven preheating while everything else is being prepped and it will be ready to go by the time it's ready to go in the oven.

Try this dish with your choice of rice! It complements the dish well and makes it more filling.

Ingredients

  • Salmon filets
  • One large lemon
  • 6-10 Brussel Sprouts (the amount depends on size of the sprouts - the larger they are, the less needed)
  • Magic Salmon seasoning
  • Olive oil for drizzling
  • Salt and pepper
